## Summary

The annual AI Index tracks the technical progress, investment, adoption, and policy landscape of artificial intelligence. The 2025 edition reports that generative AI capabilities continue to improve rapidly, model inference costs are declining, and image, video, audio, and text generation are becoming accessible to a broader set of users and organizations.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Supports the claim that generative AI dramatically lowers the cost of producing content at scale.
- claim-002: Provides background on the growing capabilities that enable more sophisticated recommendation and personalization systems.

## Key snippet

> The AI Index Report tracks, collates, distills and visualizes data relating to artificial intelligence, enabling decision-makers to take meaningful action to advance AI responsibly and ethically.
