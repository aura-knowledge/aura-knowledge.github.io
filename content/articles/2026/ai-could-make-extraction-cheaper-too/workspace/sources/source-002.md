## Summary

The World Economic Forum's Global Risks Report 2024 identifies AI-generated misinformation and disinformation as a top global risk over the near term. The report emphasizes that the scale, speed, and personalization of synthetic content can outpace societies' ability to verify information, undermining trust in elections, public health, and institutions.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-002: Supports the argument that AI-powered personalization and synthetic content make information environments harder to audit and more conducive to manipulation.
- claim-003: Provides global context for the risk of deepfakes and synthetic media entering political discourse, including in India.
