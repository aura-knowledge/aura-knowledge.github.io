## Summary

Mastodon is a free, open-source social-networking platform built on federation. It consists of thousands of independently operated servers that can communicate with one another. Users can follow accounts across servers, move accounts between servers, and choose a community whose rules they prefer.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Provides a working example of an alternative to winner-take-all platform structure, illustrating that network effects do not inevitably require a single owner.
- claim-004: Demonstrates federation in practice, where users can change providers while keeping their relationships.

## Key snippet

> Mastodon is free and open-source. It is not a single website, but a network of communities powered by a common platform. Users can follow friends and discover new ones across the network.
