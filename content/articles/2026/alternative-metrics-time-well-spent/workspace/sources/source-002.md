## Summary

In a 2018 post, then-Instagram head Adam Mosseri described the company's intention to move beyond raw time-spent metrics and optimize for "time well spent"—time that users feel good about after a session. The post notes that Instagram planned to reduce the distribution of content that receives a disproportionate number of passive views relative to meaningful interactions.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-002: Direct evidence that a major platform proposed "time well spent" and meaningful social interaction as alternative optimization targets.
- claim-003: Implicitly supports the gaming concern, since the post acknowledges the need to distinguish meaningful interaction from shallow engagement signals.
