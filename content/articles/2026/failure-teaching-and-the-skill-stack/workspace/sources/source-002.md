# source-002: Ira Glass on the taste-ability gap

## Citation

Glass, I. (n.d.). Ira Glass on the gap between taste and ability [Video]. YouTube. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BI23U7U2aUY

## Core idea

Creative people often begin with good taste but weak ability, producing work that disappoints them. The only way through the gap is to produce a large volume of work and allow ability to catch up to taste.

## Relevance to the article

Provides an accessible framing device for why early failure is normal and necessary, and why documentation of early work matters more than waiting for perfection.

## Limits

This is a creator's observation, not an empirical study; it is used as an analogy and motivational frame rather than as scientific evidence.
