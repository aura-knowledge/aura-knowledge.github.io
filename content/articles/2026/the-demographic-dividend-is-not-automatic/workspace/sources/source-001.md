## Summary

A Press Information Bureau release summarizing India's youth demographics. It notes that India has one of the world's youngest populations, with a large share of the population below 35 years of age, and frames this youth bulge as a potential demographic dividend.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Supports the statement that roughly 65% of India's population is under 35.

## Key snippet

> India has one of the world's youngest populations, with a significant share of the population below 35 years, offering a demographic dividend if harnessed through education, skill development, and employment.
