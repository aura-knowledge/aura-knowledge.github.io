## Summary

A U.S. survey report tracking parent and teen screen use, media habits, and sleep. It documents high levels of parent screen use alongside teen use and finds that families with explicit media rules and device-free routines report more balanced relationships with technology.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Parent screen use parallels child screen use.
- claim-002: Device-free rituals support family connection and better screen habits.

## Key snippet

> "Parents' own screen use is closely linked to teens' screen use, and families who set clear limits around devices report better sleep and communication."
