## Summary

UNICEF guidance for caregivers on raising children in a digital environment. It stresses warm, responsive interaction, shared activities, and age-appropriate boundaries as the foundation for healthy digital habits, rather than reliance on prohibition alone.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-003: Age-appropriate boundaries and co-viewing are preferable to bans.
- claim-004: Shared creative and conversational activities build self-regulation and attention.

## Key snippet

> "Spend time together, talk about what your child is doing online, and set a few clear rules that match your child's age and development."
