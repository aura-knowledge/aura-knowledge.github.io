## Summary

An India-focused industry report by Vivo and CyberMedia Research on how smartphones affect human relationships. It finds that Indian parents and children frequently mirror each other's screen behaviour, with children reporting feelings of being ignored when parents are absorbed in phones.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Parent and child screen behaviours mirror each other in Indian households.
- claim-002: Device-free shared time is associated with better family connection.

## Key snippet

> "A majority of respondents felt that excessive smartphone usage by a family member has impacted their relationship, with 1 in 3 Indian parents admitting that they fight with their children over phone usage."
