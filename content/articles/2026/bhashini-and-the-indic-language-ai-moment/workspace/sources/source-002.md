## Summary

Bhashini is the Indian government's Digital India language platform. It provides public datasets, translation APIs, automatic speech recognition, text-to-speech, and a marketplace of language models covering Indian languages. The platform is intended to lower the barrier for government services, startups, and researchers building Indic-language applications.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-002: Supports the claim that Bhashini has built open datasets, models, and APIs for Indian languages.
- claim-003: The translation and voice APIs reduce the cost of converting content across languages, which could help educators and builders reach wider audiences.

## Key snippet

> Bhashini aims to build a National Public Digital Platform for languages, developing open-source datasets and AI models to enable translation and speech capabilities across Indian languages.
