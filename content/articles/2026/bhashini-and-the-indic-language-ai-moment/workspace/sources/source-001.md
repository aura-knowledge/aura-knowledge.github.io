## Summary

The Internet in India Report 2024, published by IAMAI and Kantar, is one of the most widely cited industry reports on Indian internet demographics and behavior. Among its findings, the report states that 98% of Indian internet users access content in Indic languages. The report also documents continued rural growth, cheap data, and the expansion of non-English usage across services.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Provides the direct statistic that nearly all Indian internet users access Indic-language content.
- claim-004: Frames the environment in which Indic-language AI will operate—an attention economy already dominated by local-language entertainment and social content.
