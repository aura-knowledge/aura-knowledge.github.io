## Thesis

Modern AI terminology becomes less confusing when each term is explained through plain language, older related ideas, practical examples, benefits, limits, and academic roots rather than being treated as a fresh breakthrough every time. This guide introduces the AI, De-Mystified series and lists the 14 articles in order.

## Audience

- Curious builders, students, creators, and knowledge workers who use AI tools.
- Readers who want simple explanations before deeper technical detail.
- Educators and team leads introducing AI concepts to non-technical colleagues.
- Agents that need a compact overview of the series structure and article order.

## Claims

- `claim-001`: AI terminology becomes more useful when each term is explained through plain language, older related ideas, practical examples, benefits, limits, and academic roots instead of being treated as a fresh breakthrough every time.
- `claim-002`: A repeating article structure makes it easier for readers to learn one concept at a time and compare new terms with ideas they already know.
- `claim-003`: Keeping the series inside Aura Knowledge, focused on one concept at a time, protects it from becoming a broad encyclopedia, benchmark hub, or product marketing guide.
