## Summary

Khanmigo is Khan Academy's AI-powered tutoring assistant. It is designed to act as a Socratic tutor: asking follow-up questions, guiding students through problem-solving, and avoiding direct answers when possible. The product documentation emphasizes keeping the learner doing the cognitive work rather than completing assignments for them.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Supports the contrast between passive answer consumption and active problem-solving by modeling a tutor that prompts thinking.
- claim-003: Demonstrates how AI can lower friction for guided practice while preserving learner effort.
- claim-004: Illustrates the scaffold-for-struggle design philosophy.
