## Summary

The International Telecommunication Union's annual statistical report on global connectivity. It documents that lower-middle-income countries have driven recent internet growth, that mobile is the dominant access mode, and that usage in these markets skews toward communication and entertainment.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Provides the global connectivity context in which India's high mobile engagement sits.
- claim-002: Supports the comparison with Brazil, Indonesia, and Nigeria as mobile-first emerging markets.

## Key snippet

> Emerging economies in Asia, Africa, and Latin America account for a growing share of global internet users, with mobile broadband as the primary means of access.
