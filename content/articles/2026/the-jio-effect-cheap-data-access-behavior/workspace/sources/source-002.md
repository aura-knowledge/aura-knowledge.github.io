## Summary

The IAMAI-Kantar Internet in India Report 2024 estimates India's internet user base, growth trends, rural-urban split, language preferences, and usage categories. It finds that India is one of the world's largest online markets, that rural India is driving much of the remaining growth, that 98% of users access content in Indic languages, and that entertainment, social media, and messaging dominate reported use.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-002: Supports the claim that India added hundreds of millions of users and that rural growth is significant.
- claim-003: Provides the usage mix that underlies the short-form video, gaming, and messaging surge.
- claim-004: Documents that access is now near-ubiquitous while productive use remains a smaller share, framing the use question.
