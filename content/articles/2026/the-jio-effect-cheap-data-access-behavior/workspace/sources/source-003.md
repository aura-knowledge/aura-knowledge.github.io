## Summary

Bain's report examines the growth of short-form video in India, including user base size, time spent, creator economy dynamics, and platform competition. It documents how cheap data, low-cost smartphones, and Indic-language content combined to make short-form video one of the dominant uses of Indian internet time after the Jio-led price collapse.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-003: Supports the claim that low-cost data enabled a surge in short-form video consumption.

## Key snippet
