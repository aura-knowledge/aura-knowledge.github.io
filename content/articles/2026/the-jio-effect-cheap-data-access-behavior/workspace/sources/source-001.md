## Summary

TRAI's quarterly and annual performance indicators track subscriber numbers, data usage, average revenue per user (ARPU), and sector financials for Indian telecom services. The reports show a sharp inflection after Jio's September 2016 launch: data ARPU collapsed, data consumption per user surged, and the competitive landscape consolidated from many operators to a few large players.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Documents the post-2016 collapse in data prices and the corresponding rise in data consumption.
- claim-002: Provides subscriber-base and rural-urban penetration estimates that support the access-expansion narrative.

## Key snippet

> "The Performance Indicators Reports provide data on telecom subscriptions, traffic, revenue, and quality of service, including trends in wireless data usage and ARPU."
