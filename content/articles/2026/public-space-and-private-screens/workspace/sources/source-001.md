## Summary

The NSSO Time Use Survey 2024 factsheet reports that 93% of Indians aged 6 and above participate in mass media or leisure activities, and that leisure time has risen about 15% since 2019. It also records lower participation in voluntary social and community activities, providing the structural backdrop for the shift from shared public life to individualized media consumption.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Provides the core survey evidence that mass-media participation is nearly universal while voluntary social participation has declined.
- claim-002: Suggests, indirectly, that a large share of leisure and media time is now spent with personal screens in public and transit contexts.

## Key snippet
