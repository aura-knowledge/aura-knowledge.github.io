## Summary

The Centre for Internet and Society (CIS) is an Indian non-profit that produces research and advocacy on internet governance, privacy, platform regulation, and digital rights. Its work translates technical and legal developments into policy analysis relevant to Indian lawmakers, courts, and the public.

- claim-004: CIS is a key civil-society actor that converts technical platform-governance issues into public and policy discourse in India.
- claim-001: CIS research and commentary often surfaces academic and technical findings about platform harms in the Indian context.
