## Summary

UNICEF's child online safety guidance frames the internet as a space of both opportunity and risk for children. It emphasizes protection from harmful content, contact, conduct, and contract risks, including exploitative data practices and manipulative design that target children's developmental vulnerabilities.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Supports the principle that children deserve special protections online.
- claim-002: Underlines how developmental stage affects children's ability to assess risk and resist persuasion.

## Key snippet

> "Children need special protection in the digital environment, including from harmful content, exploitation, and the misuse of their personal data."
