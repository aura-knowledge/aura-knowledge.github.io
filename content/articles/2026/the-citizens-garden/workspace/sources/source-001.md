## Summary

Bhashini is the Indian government's AI-driven language platform under the Digital India program. It aims to make digital services accessible in Indian languages through translation, automatic speech recognition (ASR), text-to-speech (TTS), and natural-language understanding models. The platform supports multiple Indic languages and is positioned as infrastructure for government, business, and citizen use.
