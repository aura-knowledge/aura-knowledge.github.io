## Summary

A government-backed report estimating the size and composition of India's digital economy. It places the digital economy at roughly 11.74% of GDP and highlights the dominant role of services and consumer platforms.

## Relevance to claims

- claim-001: Provides the GDP share estimate and the services-led composition.
- claim-002: Contextualizes the consumer-platform economy within the broader digital-economy estimate.

## Key snippet

> India's digital economy is estimated at 11.74% of GDP, with digital services and platform ecosystems contributing the largest shares of value added.
